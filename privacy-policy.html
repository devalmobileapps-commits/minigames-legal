📋 Quick Summary

GamePocket is a privacy-first application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data. The app operates completely offline and all data remains on your device.

1

Definitions and Scope

1.1 Definitions

  • "Personal Information" means any information that can be used to identify an individual person
  • "User" means any person who downloads, installs, accesses, or uses the App
  • "Device" means any mobile device, tablet, or computer used to access the App
  • "Local Storage" means data stored solely on the User's Device

1.2 Scope of Application

This Policy applies to all users of GamePocket, regardless of geographical location, and governs our privacy practices in connection with your use of our App.

2

Information We Do NOT Collect

GamePocket is designed with privacy-by-design principles. We do not collect, access, store, transmit, or process any personal information or user data, including but not limited to:

2.1 Personal Identifiers

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers
  • Social media profiles or account information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Government-issued identification numbers

2.2 Device and Technical Information

  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, Advertising ID)
  • IP addresses or network information
  • Device specifications or operating system details
  • App usage statistics or analytics data

2.3 Location and Behavioral Data

  • Precise or approximate location information
  • User behavior patterns or gameplay analytics
  • Search history or preferences
  • Communication or contact information

2.4 Multimedia Content

  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Contacts or calendar information
  • File system access beyond app sandbox
3

Local Data Storage

GamePocket may store limited, non-personal data locally on your Device solely for application functionality. This local storage is managed by your Device's operating system and never transmitted externally.

3.1 Types of Local Data

  • Game Preferences: User interface settings and theme preferences
  • Game Progress: High scores, achievement progress, and game statistics
  • Application Settings: Sound preferences and difficulty levels

3.2 Data Control

Users maintain full control over locally stored data and can clear it at any time through:

  • App settings within GamePocket
  • Device application management settings
  • Uninstalling and reinstalling the application
4

App Permissions and Security

4.1 Minimal Permissions

GamePocket requests only the minimum permissions necessary for core functionality:

  • Storage Access: Limited to app-specific data storage
  • Network State: Only to detect connectivity for offline verification

4.2 No Sensitive Permissions

We do NOT request access to:

  • Camera, microphone, or location services
  • Contacts, SMS, or call logs
  • External storage or file systems
  • Device admin or system-level permissions
5

Third-Party Services and Integrations

Zero Third-Party Integration

GamePocket operates in complete isolation and does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks or monetization platforms
  • Social media platforms or sharing services
  • Cloud storage or backup services
  • Crash reporting or diagnostic tools
  • Authentication or user account services
6

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

6.1 Child-Safe Design

GamePocket is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children under 13 years old. Since we do not collect any personal information, COPPA requirements are inherently satisfied.

6.2 Parental Guidance

While no personal data is collected, we recommend parental supervision for:

  • Managing screen time and gaming duration
  • Ensuring appropriate break intervals
  • Monitoring device temperature during extended play
7

Health and Safety Guidelines

⚠️ Important Health and Safety Information

Please read carefully before using GamePocket:

  • Eye Health: Take 15-minute breaks every hour to prevent eye strain
  • Posture: Maintain proper posture and avoid prolonged hunched positions
  • Device Temperature: Stop playing if your device becomes uncomfortably warm
  • Environmental Safety: Do not play while driving, walking, or operating machinery
  • Photosensitivity: Discontinue use if you experience seizures or discomfort from flashing lights
  • Repetitive Strain: Take breaks to prevent repetitive stress injuries
